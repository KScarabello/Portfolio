Lend A Hand

Awarded "Best Solution for a Business"

Lend A Hand is a crowd-resourcing platform following in the spirit of GoFundMe and KickStarter, except you do not donate money, but rather your time and dedication. The technologies used are React, React-Redux, SASS, MomentJS, Material-UI, Node JS (with Express), PostgreSQL (with Massive), Auth0.

Maintenance Minder

Maintenance Minder is an application that allows users to keep records of all maintenance activities performed on their assets, as well as receive reminders for any upcoming scheduled maintenance needs. This project was completed by a team of 5 DevMountain students as a capstone group project.
