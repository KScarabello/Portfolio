Hi! I'm Kim. I love creating beautiful and functional things with Javascript code.

I currently reside in Provo, Utah, but call many different places home.

My perfect Friday night would involve my computer, some healthy snacks, and a good tutorial for the next piece of technology on my wish list.

When not in front of a computer, I am passionate about the pursuit of adventurous and unique life experiences. Some of my favorites thus far have been marching in the Macy's Thanksgiving Day parade, living in Death Valley National Park for 6 weeks in a tent, and helping to reinsulate 34 mobile homes damaged during the Hurricane Sandy relief effort. I have stories galore.

I'm a classically-trained violinist, who began playing at age 5. I was raised to play Bach and Mozart, but my dream would be to play like Boyd Tinsley (Dave Matthews Band). I'll get there someday.
